As an Audit Senior, you will play a crucial role in planning, executing, and completing audit engagements for a variety of clients across different industries. You will be responsible for leading audit teams, reviewing work papers, and ensuring compliance with relevant accounting standards and regulations. This is an excellent opportunity to further develop your technical skills, leadership abilities, and client management expertise in a supportive and challenging environment.

*   Plan and execute audit engagements in accordance with auditing standards (e.g., IFRS, GAAP).
*   Lead and supervise audit teams, providing guidance and mentorship to junior staff.
*   Review audit work papers to ensure accuracy, completeness, and compliance with professional standards.
*   Identify and assess audit risks and develop appropriate audit procedures.
*   Perform substantive testing and analytical procedures to evaluate the fairness of financial statements.
*   Prepare and review financial statements, including footnotes and disclosures.
*   Communicate audit findings and recommendations to clients and management.
*   Maintain strong client relationships and provide exceptional client service.
*   Stay up-to-date on relevant accounting and auditing standards and regulations.
*   Manage audit budgets and timelines to ensure engagements are completed efficiently and effectively.
*   Contribute to the development and improvement of audit methodologies and processes.
*   Work collaboratively with a team of 8 professionals, including 2 managers and 1 partner, handling a broad client base across multiple sectors including financial services, manufacturing, and tech startups.
